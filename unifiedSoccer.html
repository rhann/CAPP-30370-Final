About Unified Soccer

Unified Soccer is an event hosted by the Notre Dame Special Olympics Club. A Notre Dame soccer team is put together composed of both Special Olympic Athletes and Notre Dame students. A tournament is then held in the Spring where the Unified team competes. At the tournament, Notre Dame's Unified Team competes against teams from the University of Michigan and Central Michigan in friendly competition. This event allows students to build relationships with athletes form the South Bend and Michiana communities.

If you would like to participate in this event, please go to our Get Involved page to leave your contact information. We are always in need of volunteers and athletes to compete!
