About Spread the Word to End the Word

Spread the Word to End the Word is a national campaign to end the use of the word ‘retard(ed)’. The campaign asks people to pledge to stop saying the R–word as a starting point toward creating more accepting attitudes and communities for all people. Language affects attitudes and attitudes affect actions.

The Notre Dame Special Olympics Club reaches out to the Notre Dame Community to support the end of the use of the R–word. The club collects signatures of students who pledge refrain from the use of the R–word. The club also reaches out to the community surrounding Notre Dame for support of the campaign. The club travels to local high schools to collect signatures.

The club also sells t–shirts to raise money for the cause. These shirts are sold at the LaFortune Student Center and Dinning Halls during the Spread the Word to End the Word week each spring.
